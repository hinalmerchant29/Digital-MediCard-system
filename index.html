Digital MediCard System

A Unified, Secure, and Centralized Digital Health Record Platform

Problem Statement

Fragmented medical records across hospitals and clinics lead to poor accessibility, duplication of tests, and delays in treatment.

Our Solution

Digital MediCard provides a centralized digital health identity that securely stores and manages patient medical history, accessible to authorized healthcare providers.

Key Features

  • Unified digital medical record
  • Secure role-based access
  • Centralized patient database
  • Improved healthcare efficiency

Future Scope

Future enhancements include integration with hospital databases, cloud-based storage implementation, Aadhaar-based health ID linkage, and AI-powered analytics for predictive healthcare insights.
